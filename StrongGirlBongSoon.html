No Spoilers:

Strong Girl Do Bong Soon" is a romantic-comedy and superhero K-drama with plenty of fun and action. It follows the story of Do Bong Soon, a woman born with superhuman strength, as she navigates life, love, and crime-fighting.
She dreams of creating a video game based on herself but ends up working as a bodyguard for Ahn Min Hyuk, the CEO of a gaming company. Along the way, there's romance, mystery, and a quirky love triangle, with hilarious and heartwarming moments.

My Opinion:

Strong Woman Do Bong Soon is a delightful South Korean drama that seamlessly blends action, comedy, and romance. Park Bo Young delivers a stellar performance as the titular character, a woman with superhuman strength who finds herself caught up in a kidnapping case while working as a bodyguard for a quirky CEO. The show's charm lies in its lighthearted tone and the endearing chemistry between the leads. Park Hyung Sik shines as the eccentric CEO who falls head over heels for Bong Soon's unique personality. The supporting cast, including Ji Soo as Bong Soon's childhood friend, adds depth and humor to the narrative. While the plot occasionally veers into melodrama, the overall effect is a feel-good experience that will leave you smiling. Strong Woman Do Bong Soon is a refreshing take on the superhero genre, offering a heartwarming and entertaining escape.
